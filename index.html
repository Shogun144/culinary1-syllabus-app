Course Description

Find out if the Culinary Arts industry is for you in this hands-on class where foundation standards are stressed. Students will gain practical experience and theoretical knowledge essential for a career in the culinary field. The course focuses on industry standards, including obtaining food handler, CPR, and First Aid certifications. Key topics include knife skills, commercial kitchen operations, understanding food leavening, and in-depth nutritional concepts. Emphasis will also be placed on applied mathematical skills such as metric versus standard measurements, scale operations, baker‚Äôs percentages, food cost determination, knife sharpening angles, graphing with fats and oils, and change counting.

Learning Goals

  • Apply foundational culinary industry standards.
  • Obtain food handler, CPR, and First Aid certifications.
  • Master knife skills and safe handling.
  • Work effectively in a commercial kitchen.
  • Explain principles of food leavening.
  • Analyze and apply nutritional concepts.
  • Utilize math for measurements, costing, and graphing.
  • Demonstrate proficiency in practical culinary techniques.
  • Participate in real-world culinary events.
